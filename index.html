BID IMPACT PROGRAM

BID is organizing an impact program to cultivate the quick adoption of new and emerging technologies and infrastructures in the upcoming era. The whole process is going to proceed in such a way that the companies & startups will list the problems and issues they are facing in their workflow and the enthusiastic contributors will resolve their obstacles by discovering simple solutions and helping them in their growth and development. There will be some mentors who will guide and take care of the whole process.
This BID Impact program is organized to bridge the deep understanding of the potential of a whole community and how can we grow together.
